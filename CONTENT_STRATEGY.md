# Content Strategy

## Positioning

Behind The Smile speaks about Ukraine, service members, veterans, trauma, pain, mental health, rehabilitation, and access to prescribed care.

The account is not about promoting a specific medicine. It is about dignity, safe treatment, and the right to professional help.

## Core Message

People who went through war may face pain, anxiety, insomnia, PTSD symptoms, exhaustion, substance risks, or complicated recovery.

They deserve access to qualified care without stigma. Medicines must be prescribed and monitored by clinicians. Self-medication is unsafe.

## Pillars

- Access to prescribed treatment for military personnel, veterans, and civilians affected by war.
- Anti-stigma around psychiatric, psychological, pain, and addiction care.
- Responsible medicine use: doctor supervision, no self-medication, no sharing pills.
- Family support: notice suffering without shame, pressure, or moralizing.
- Rehabilitation as strength, not weakness.
- Behind The Smile brand and community support as a softer emotional pillar.

## Safety Rules

- Do not give dosages.
- Do not explain how to obtain controlled medicines.
- Do not recommend pregabalin or any other medicine to a person.
- Do not frame medicine as a universal solution.
- Do not shame addiction, relapse, psychiatric care, or pain.
- Always keep the balance: access matters, but only through professional care.

## Recommended Cadence

- Threads: 3-4 posts per day.
- X: 1-2 posts per day using prepared composer links.
- Rotate topics so medical posts do not appear as a single-issue campaign.

## Example Weekly Mix

- 30% access to care and policy framing.
- 25% anti-stigma and mental health support.
- 20% responsible medicine use and self-medication warnings.
- 15% family and community support.
- 10% Behind The Smile or softer emotional posts.
